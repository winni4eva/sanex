FedEx Compatible Tier Program
Many software solutions have earned placement in the FedEx Compatible Tier Program. This program recognizes software solutions that bring the latest FedEx services to our customers, as well as work well with FedEx field employees to help our mutual customers. Although all FedEx Compatible software solutions are highly recommended by FedEx, those solutions that earn a place in the FedEx Compatible Tier Program have gone above and beyond to exemplify the program standards.
