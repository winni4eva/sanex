FedEx Billing Online

Be more efficient in your job — and business — with FedEx Billing Online. This powerful solution provides the transparency, control and insight you need to meet your company’s requirements.

Benefits

  • Flexible. Adapt the features and functionality to your business, not the other way around.
  • Efficient. Instantly view, pay, dispute and manage invoices from one location.
  • Print, download, save. Manage just the invoices you need the way you need them.
  • Payment options. Pay by electronic funds transfer, paper check, debit card or credit card.
  • Account management. Set up auto-pay and notifications to help you manage your payables your way.
  • Digital security. Feel confident with our secure, password-protected site.

Credit Card Billed Accounts

No invoicing. No late payments. No hassle. Link your FedEx account to a credit card that will be billed automatically.
