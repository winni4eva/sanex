FedEx Express MultiweightÂ® pricing: for multiple-piece shipments

To help you save money, FedEx automatically offers multiweight pricing on FedEx ExpressÂ®Â U.S. and international services. With U.S. multiweight pricing, multiple-piece shipments traveling from one origin to one destination, and totaling 100 lbs. or more (200 lbs. or more for FedEx Express SaverÂ®Â shipments) are rated on a price-per-package basis and on a price-per-total-shipment-weight basis. With international multiweight pricing, multiple-piece shipments traveling from one origin to one destination are rated on a price-per-total-shipment-weight basis. Per-pound rates apply if the total shipment weight is 100 lbs. or more.
