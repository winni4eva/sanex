Delivery Time

Special Instructions

To lessen the possibility of damage or delay, follow these packaging guidelines when preparing your express freight shipment. An Extra Services Charge will be assessed if your shipment does not meet these guidelines:

  • Individual pieces over 150 lbs. should be banded to the pallet with either metal strapping or unbreakable plastic straps applied around the box or skid freight on all sides.
  • Hardened containers (plywood, metal) that are not banded should have an exterior lock or clamp (glue, nails and screws are not sufficient).

Exceptions

Shipments to and from extended service areas may be picked up and/or delivered by a cartage agent. FedEx Express Multiweight® pricing: for multiple-piece shipments

To help you save money, FedEx automatically offers multiweight pricing on FedEx Express® U.S. and international services. With U.S. multiweight pricing, multiple-piece shipments traveling from one origin to one destination, and totaling 100 lbs. or more (200 lbs. or more for FedEx Express Saver® shipments) are rated on a price-per-package basis and on a price-per-total-shipment-weight basis. You are charged the lower of the two rates. A 15-lb. average minimum package weight for the shipment applies.

With international multiweight pricing, multiple-piece shipments traveling from one origin to one destination are rated on a price-per-total-shipment-weight basis. Per-pound rates apply if the total shipment weight is 100 lbs. or more.

Single-piece shipments weighing more than 100 lbs. may also be subject to multiweight pricing.

For additional information, see Multiple-Piece Shipments.


Package and Shipment Size

Standard U.S. and international package limitations and shipping terms apply.
 

Multiweight Benefits

  • Shipments are supported by a money-back guarantee.
  • Shipments do not require palletizing, shrinkwrapping, staging or shipment segregation.
  • Multiweight pricing is available for both regular and on-call pickups.
  • With multiweight pricing, you can ship up to 999 packages on one airbill.
  • Multiple packages in one shipment may have different weights, dimensions and declared values.
  • Multiweight pricing is available for shipments to Alaska and Hawaii, depending on the selected FedEx Express service.
  • Saturday delivery is available for shipments traveling via FedEx Priority Overnight®, FedEx 2Day® and FedEx International Priority®.


Multiweight pricing is offered with the following U.S. services:

FedEx First Overnight®

FedEx Priority Overnight®

FedEx Standard Overnight®

FedEx 2Day® A.M.

FedEx 2Day®

FedEx Express Saver®


Multiweight pricing is offered with the following international services:

FedEx International First®

FedEx International Priority®

FedEx International Economy®

See FedEx Express Multiweight rates in the FedEx Service Guide.

FedEx Freight®: time-definite services

Service Details

Delivery Times

A.M. Delivery: delivery by 10:30 a.m. on the standard delivery date (where available). An up-charge applies.

Close of Business Delivery: delivery by 5 p.m. on the standard delivery date for an up-charge (except for standard tariff customers using FedEx Freight Priority, who receive a no-fee money-back guarantee).

See A.M. Delivery and Close of Business Delivery in the FXF 100 Series Rules Tariff for up-charge information.

  

Service Days

Monday–Friday.

  

Delivery Area

A.M. Delivery: Check your destination ZIP code eligibility at fedex.com/amfreight.

Close of Business Delivery: Available throughout the 48 contiguous states.

  

Additional Information

Simply check A.M. Delivery or Close of Business Delivery on your Bill of Lading, or note it within your Bill of Lading special instructions and inform your FedEx Freight driver.

Services are subject to the terms and conditions provided in the FXF 100 Series Rules Tariff.

  

Options

FedEx Ground Multiweight®: bulk ground shipments

Service Details

Service Description

Ideal for multiple-package shipments weighing 200 lbs. or more moving as one unit to the same destination on the same day. This pricing option allows you to combine packages for a multiweight rate. Pricing is based on the combined weight of your packages.

  

Delivery Times

Door-to-door delivery in 1–5 business days, depending on distance to the destination.

Delivery to Alaska and Hawaii in 3–7 business days.

  

Service Days

Monday–Friday.

  

Delivery Area

Available throughout all 50 states.

  

Package Size and Weight

Multiple-piece shipments with a total weight of 200 lbs. or more can qualify.

Average package weight should be 15–25 lbs.

Up to 150 lbs. per package, 108" in length, 165" in length plus girth (L+2W+2H).

  

Additional Information

Service includes inside delivery and up to three delivery attempts.

Shrinkwrapping or palletizing shipments is not required.

Packages are processed like any other FedEx Ground® shipment.

Contract pricing is required.

  

How to Use

To see if you qualify, contact your FedEx account executive.

FedEx® Charters: use of an entire aircraft

Service Details

Service Description

This service allows you to full-charter a FedEx aircraft. You choose the origin and destination airports, with airport-to-airport service available in the U.S. and internationally. We provide build and break of cargo, load and unload of cargo, a professional loadmaster for the flight, and 24-hour access to FedEx Charters Flight Operations and our sales representatives.

Delivery Area

Available in the U.S. and internationally for one-time or long-term use.

Additional Information

FedEx Charters specializes in general cargo; hard-to-move cargo; and animals, including zoo animals, horses and cattle. Individualized security options and temperature-sensitive cargo environments are also available. For more information, go to the FedEx Charters website.

How to Use

For more information or to request a quote, email chartersales@fedex.com, or call 1.800.238.0181 (customers in the U.S. and Canada), 1.901.224.6500 (customers in South America) or (49)69.69810.135 (customers in Europe and Asia).
