Shipment Packaging: FedEx Can Pack Almost Anything


You know that you can ship almost anything with FedEx, but do you know what shipment packaging should be used to help ensure your item arrives in the same condition in which it was packed? Some items can seem difficult to pack, but at FedEx Office, we can easily pack just about anything you need to ship. See our list of common items here.

Come into a FedEx Office location and visit the Pack Plus counter if you have something that you feel is beyond your shipment packing capabilities. Our trained professionals have the shipping packaging, the experience and the know-how to pack even heavy and oddly shaped items. Plus, everything we pack is backed by the FedEx Office® Packing Pledge, so you can rest assured that your item will arrive safely.
