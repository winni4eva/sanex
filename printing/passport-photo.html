Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to apply for an expedited passport?

Standard passport processing time for the Department of State is 4-6 weeks. If you are planning to travel in less than four weeks, you will need expedited services. Certain services require proof of travel or upcoming travel itinerary to qualify for an expedited passport. A checklist at the end of the application process will let you know what additional documentation you may need to provide, including:

  • Your plane tickets

  • A travel itinerary

  • Your current or expired passport book if available

  • Proof of citizenship (birth certificate or naturalization certificate)

  • A personal or company check for your required government fee

  • A business letter on your company’s letterhead, indicating your business travel plans, travel dates, and relationship to the company, addressed to the Department of State

  • A letter from the Human Resources Department of your company substantiating your need for a passport for employment purposes, printed on a company letterhead addressed to the Department of State

How long does it take to get an expedited passport or rush a passport renewal?

Your passport application will be processed based on the service you select at the time of application. Our services include:

  • 24-hour service — 1 business day

  • Next-day service — 2 business days

  • Priority service — 3–5 business days

  • Rush service — 6–7 business days

  • Standard service — 8–10 business days

  • Rush renewal* — 10–12 business days
    *Renewals only.

Please note that this indicates processing time, not necessarily when you will receive your passport. Shipping times must be factored in as well. For example, by choosing the 24-hour service option on Monday, FedEx will receive your application and begin processing Tuesday morning. By Tuesday afternoon, your passport will be shipped to you for Wednesday delivery. You may be eligible to pick up your passport Tuesday afternoon or have it delivered the same day in select cities.

Is FedEx Office a passport acceptance facility?

No. While our services can help you take and print passport photos, ship a passport application at FedEx Office and expedite your applications, we ultimately cannot accept passports. To learn more about how your passport application will be processed, apply for your passport here with FedEx, and our specialists will assist you with any questions.

How recent should my photo be?

A government-compliant passport photo must be taken within the past six months and reflect your current appearance.

Where can I find more information about how to apply for a U.S. passport?

For more information on applying for a U.S. passport, and to complete a U.S. passport application online, ask our passport specialists while you apply online through FedEx. They’re available to help you with your application 24/7. To have a government-compliant passport photo taken and printed, or to print a passport application, find a FedEx Office location near you.

When should I renew my passport?

You should renew your passport about nine months before it expires. Most countries require that your passport validity be at least six months beyond the dates of your trip. Some airlines will not allow you to board if this requirement is not met. For more information on how to renew a passport quickly, apply online through FedEx. Our passport specialists will be available to answer any questions 24/7.
