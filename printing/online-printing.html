Frequently Asked Questions

What is online printing?

Online printing is a convenient way to get your printing done quickly and efficiently using your desktop computer, laptop, tablet or smartphone. You can use the online tool to print documents, flyers, presentations, banners, brochures, posters, manuals, signs, and more for your business or personal use. When you choose online printing services, you can pick up your order at a FedEx Office location or get free shipping on your order of $100 or more.

Where can you print documents anywhere, anytime?

Where can you print documents anywhere, anytime?

Get convenient access to FedEx Office® Print Online when you’re wondering where to print documents, or want options for where to get documents printed, no matter where you travel. Choose one of our professional project templates, which you can then personalize with your company name, logo, and other messaging. FedEx Office provides more places to print documents on demand.

Where can I make copies?

Want to know where to make photocopies or where to get copies made? Visit a FedEx Office location for a place to print documents quickly and efficiently. Find out where to print color copies quickly by working with a FedEx Office team member for full-service copying or using the self-serve copiers at your convenience.

Where can I print an email?

When you need to quickly print an email attachment or want to find places to print documents, we have you covered. With FedEx Office® Print & Go, you can email the attachment to printandgo@fedex.com. You will receive a retrieval code for your order, which you can enter at the Print & Go payment device near the self-service printer at FedEx Office. Find a location.

How much is it to print at FedEx Office?

With options to fit any budget, the cost of FedEx Office® printing services varies according to media (paper stock, vinyl, plastic, aluminum, and more) and quantity. Our online printing tool calculates the cost of services as you design and print your document or project. You can pick up your order at a FedEx Office location, or your order of $100 or more ships free. Get started with your project now.

Does FedEx Office offer an online print coupon?

Yes. We offer an online coupon for printing services. You can print the coupon and take it to a FedEx Office location or use the associated promo code when you order online. Promotions change periodically. Shipping is free on orders of $100 or more, or you can pick up your order at a FedEx Office location.
