Frequently Asked Questions


What is a canvas print?

A canvas print is a printed photo or image onto canvas, which is stretched around a wooden frame. If you are looking for where to get canvas prints that feature your favorite photo, visit your local FedEx Office or start an online order now.

Where do I go to find out how to print on canvas?

We can help you when you’re wondering how to make canvas prints or want to turn canvas prints into decorative keepsakes. Order online or visit a nearby FedEx Office location to order personalized canvas prints. 

What are the pickup and delivery options?
  • Order a poster or canvas print online
  • Pick up posters and canvas prints in-store
  • Get free ground shipping on orders over $100
  • Most orders are ready in less than 24 hours
How much are canvases and how much does it cost to print a poster?
  • Prices for canvas prints start at $34.99.*
  • Prices for posters start at $21.75*
  • Get a quote on your specific project at a FedEx Office location.

 

*For complete pricing details, see sizes & pricing above.
