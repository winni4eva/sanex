Online Printing - FAQs

Online Printing - FAQs

REGISTRATION


Why should I register?

Why should I register?

Registration is optional, but there are many great benefits to being a registered user

  • Get promotions
  • Re-order from past orders
  • Save documents in your account with My Online Documents
  • Save incomplete orders for later
  • Complete your order faster with Express Checkout
How do I reset my password?

1.       Go to the Print Online home page at https://www.fedex.com/apps/printonline/#!

2.       Select the Sign Up or Log In link in the purple header.

3.       Select the Forgot Password field. The Password Recovery box will appear.

4.       Enter the User ID which was created during the registration process and select the Continue button.

5.       Answer the two security reminder questions which were answered during the registration process or select the Request Code button. If you cannot answer the question, you may create a new user ID and password following the normal registration process.

6.       You will be prompted to enter your new password. An email will be sent informing you that your password has been changed.

7.       If you select the Request Code button, you will receive an email with a validation code which will allow you to reset your password. Click on the validation code link in the email. This will direct you to the Password Recovery screen where you can enter your new password. The validation code/link is only valid for 24 hours.

How do I retrieve my User ID?

1.       Go to the Print Online home page at https://www.fedex.com/apps/printonline/#!

2.       Select the Sign Up or Log In link in the purple header.

3.       Select the Forgot your User ID field below the LOGIN button. The User ID Recovery box will appear.

4.       Enter the email address which was used during the registration process. Click OK.

5.       The user ID will be emailed to you. 



CREATE, PREVIEW, PRINT


Which file types are supported?

For large format printing, like signs, banners, and posters, the following additional file types will also be accepted.

.pg, .ps, .gif, .jpeg, .jpe, .dib, .jp2, .jff, .tiff, .tif, .bmp, png

You can upload non-standard files to FedEx Office® Print Online. But, no preview or pricing will be available. You will be able to enter special instructions. A team member will contact you to confirm the details of your order.

What is the maximum file size accepted?

150 MB per file.

Can I upload and combine files of mixed page orientation?

When you upload files of mixed orientation, FedEx Office® Print Online automatically combines your files based on the orientation of the first uploaded file. FedEx Office Print Online will not allow you to keep mixed page orientations, but this information can be entered into Special Instructions.

What if I don’t see the feature I need?

Click the “Add Special Instructions” link to type in your instructions. A team member will contact you after you submit your order to review your instructions, confirm pricing, and order completion time.

Will you print copyrighted files?

FedEx Office Terms and Conditions prohibit the printing of any materials that are the subject of, or which infringe upon, any patent, trademark, trade name, trade secret, copyright, right of publicity, moral right or other intellectual property right of another person or entity. You are required to check the Terms and Conditions box on the Order Confirmation page, indicating that you have read and agree to the FedEx Office Terms and Conditions, before placing your order.

Can I save an order and return later to finish setting it up?

Yes. If you are registered and signed in, and forget to save your documents to your My Online Documents, FedEx Office®Print Online will automatically save your unfinished orders for you to work on later.


Deliver


How do I receive my order?

Pick up your order at one of our nationwide locations, or have it delivered using FedEx® shipping or local delivery.

How can I get my order faster?

FedEx Office® Print Online provides you with an estimated completion time and date based on the printing and options you selected. You may call the center to see whether they can produce your order in less time.

Which shipping options are available in FedEx Office® Print Online?

FedEx First Overnight®, FedEx Priority Overnight®, FedEx Standard Overnight®, FedEx 2Day®, FedEx Express Saver®, FedEx Ground® and FedEx Home Delivery®.

Is FedEx Office® local delivery available at all FedEx Office locations?

No, it’s available at select locations to destinations within a 30-mile radius. Orders with special instructions cannot be delivered via this service.


Pay


How can I pay for my order?
  • American Express, VISA, MasterCard, and Discover
  • FedEx Office account, which can be used for printing
  • FedEx account, which can be used for shipping
How can I view or make a payment to my FedEx Office account online?

Login or register for ebilling by visiting Billing Online. When enrolled, ebilling allows you to view statements, obtain electronic copies of invoices and pay bills.

Can I use FedEx Office® Print Online for my tax-exempt order?

Yes. Indicate your tax-exempt status on the “Add Special Instructions” page, accessible from a link on the Set Print Options page. For frequent tax-exempt orders, contact a center manager to learn how to apply for a FedEx Office Commercial Account.

How can I get a receipt for my online order?
  • After placing your order, the Thank You for Your Order page appears. Print this page as your receipt.
  • If you are signed in, when placing your order, visit the Order History to view and print details about your orders, or use your order confirmation email as a receipt.
How do I redeem a promo code?

There are two ways to enter a promo code

  • On the cart page underneath your items
  • On the checkout page underneath your order summary
What is Express Checkout?

As a registered user, FedEx Office will use your preferred pick-up location and default payment to make your checkout process fast and easy.


TRACK


How do I track my order?

There are two ways that you can track your order: Sign in, highlight the order from your order history and click “Track Order” or enter your order number on the Order Status & Tracking page.

Where is my order number?

When you place a FedEx Office® Print Online order, you receive a confirmation email. This email contains your unique order number, called a GTN.


REUSE


How do I reprint an order?
  • When ordering, register and sign in to reprint in the future from your Order history: Click on the “View Past Orders” link
  • Check off the box next to the job you wish to reprint and click the “Reorder” button
  • Following the on-screen instructions, change any printing, binding and shipping options, or simply submit the order as is.
Can I change the content of an order before reprinting it?
  • When ordering, register and sign in to reprint in the future from your Order history: Click on the “View Past Orders” link
  • Check off the box next to the job you wish to reprint and click the “Reorder” button
  • Following the on-screen instructions, change any printing, binding and shipping options, or simply submit the order as is.


MY ONLINE DOCUMENTS:


What are the benefits of saving my documents?
  • 24/7 anywhere, anytime access
  • Save time and be efficient: Save your print and finishing options and order the same document as many times as you need
  • Free and unlimited online storage: Save your most frequently used print materials, organized and readily available for printing
How long are documents available in My Online Documents?

Documents saved in My Online Documents do not expire.
