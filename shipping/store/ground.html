Frequently Asked Questions About FedEx Ground® Shipping
How long do FedEx Ground shipments take to arrive?

Delivery takes 1–5 business days within the continental 48 states, depending on the distance. Shipments to Alaska, Hawaii and Canada arrive in 3–7 business days. Delivery is day-definite, meaning by the end of the scheduled delivery day. See how fast your shipment will arrive with our service tool map. 

What size boxes are available?

We have boxes of all shapes and sizes. Our pros are experts at finding the right box and creating the perfect custom packing solution. See packing and shipping options.

Can I use the white FedEx Express® packaging for my FedEx Ground shipment?

No, the white boxes are for FedEx Express shipments only. Brown boxes can be purchased for FedEx Ground shipments. Learn more about our packing services.

How do I ship a return with FedEx Ground?

The easiest way is to bring your item to one of our nearby FedEx Office locations, and a team member will handle everything for you. See tips for return shipping.
