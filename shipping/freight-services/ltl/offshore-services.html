Alaska

Simplify and Streamline

From pickup to delivery, FedEx Freight offers the ease and convenience of working with one carrier for all your Alaska shipments. That means one phone call, one Bill of Lading and one website. Plus, all ground and ocean charges are incorporated into one invoice to simplify your paperwork.

Enjoy Speed and Flexibility

With reliable on-time delivery and comprehensive regional and extended fast-transit and economical freight service throughout the U.S., FedEx Freight gets your products to the ports of exit quickly and reliably. Multiple sailings, combined with fast inland transit times, give you the flexibility to manage your shipping schedule based on the needs of your customers.

FedEx Freight® service to Alaska provides you with:

  • Comprehensive Alaska coverage
  • Multiple departures and flexible deliveries
  • Single-carrier convenience
  • Fast inland transit times across all 48 contiguous states
  • Online access to shipment information 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
  • Consolidation service available
  • Full-container-load (FCL) capabilities
  • Local, knowledgeable staff
  • Shipment-status tracking

Puerto Rico

Simplify and Streamline

Whether you're shipping from San Jose to San Juan or Portland to Ponce, reaching your customers in Puerto Rico is seamless with door-to-door, less-than-container-load (LCL) service from FedEx Freight.

Enjoy Speed and Reliability

Rely on us for the ease and convenience of dealing with one carrier for all your Puerto Rico shipments. Enjoy fast transit times, complete island coverage with multiple weekly sailings, reliable delivery and simplified paperwork.

FedEx Freight® service to Puerto Rico provides you with:

  • Quick, reliable door-to-door service
  • One single invoice and Bill of Lading
  • Complete island coverage
  • Timely northbound and southbound service with multiple weekly ocean carrier sailings
  • Bilingual customer service and a knowledgeable local staff
  • Same-day pickup available in the U.S. and in the U.S. territory of Puerto Rico
  • Online access to shipment information 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
  • Single-carrier convenience
  • Shipment-status tracking

U.S. Virgin Islands

Enjoy Speed and Reliability

Rely on us for the ease and convenience of dealing with one carrier for your inbound shipments to the U.S. Virgin Islands. Enjoy fast transit times and direct delivery to the port of destination with multiple weekly sailings, reliable delivery and simplified paperwork. FedEx Freight provides less-than-container-load (LCL) and full-container-load (FCL) service to the U.S. Virgin Islands.

FedEx Freight® service to the U.S. Virgin Islands provides you with:

  • One single invoice and Bill of Lading
  • Single-carrier convenience
  • Coverage from the U.S., Canada, Mexico and Puerto Rico
  • Multiple weekly ocean carrier sailings
  • Inbound shipping to the port of destination*
  • Online access to shipment information 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
  • Shipment-status tracking
  • LCL and FCL capabilities
  • Ground portion of shipment rates based on National Motor Freight Classification (NMFC); ocean portion of shipment rates based on per hundredweight (CWT).
