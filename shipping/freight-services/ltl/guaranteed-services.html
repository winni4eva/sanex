FedEx Freight Priority Plus: Fast Delivery for Urgent Freight

Get your urgent freight shipment to its destination quickly with FedEx Freight Priority Plus.

FedEx Freight® Priority has the fastest published transit times of any nationwide LTL service. With FedEx Freight Priority Plus, you add even more speed for time-sensitive shipments within the U.S.

Unexpected change? No problem. You can request to upgrade to this service even if your shipment is already in transit.

Added Speed

Each FedEx Freight Priority Plus shipment is optimized to travel with specialized routing options — all geared to speed up your delivery.

You can get added assurance that your critical freight shipments will arrive on time by taking advantage of time-definite delivery windows not covered through typical A.M. delivery, such as early morning, non-business hours and even Saturday delivery.

Plus, our team of transportation specialists is on hand to help you determine the best way to speed up the delivery of your shipment.

Trusted Reliability

With FedEx Freight Priority Plus, you get end-to-end visibility so you can easily and confidently track the status of your shipments. And your shipment is backed by our money-back guarantee.*

Fast shipping options and complete, reliable service.

A.M. Delivery: Early Delivery and Peace of Mind

Do you plan labor and manufacturing schedules to the minute? If so, choose our A.M. Delivery service for delivery by 10:30 a.m. on the scheduled delivery date.1

Details About the Service

  • Available with both FedEx Freight® Priority and FedEx Freight® Economy services.
  • See the FXF 100 Series Rules Tariff for applicable surcharges.
  • Supported by a money-back guarantee.2

Choose from two easy options to request A.M. Delivery for your next shipment:

  • When using a FedEx Freight Bill of Lading, check A.M. Delivery under Guaranteed Service Options.
  • Clearly write “A.M. Delivery” in the special instructions of your Bill of Lading and inform your FedEx driver of the earlier-delivery request.

Is Your Freight Shipment Eligible for A.M. Delivery?

To check your destination ZIP code eligibility, go to fedex.com/amfreight.

Close of Business Delivery: Get Delivery by 5 p.m.

When you need extra assurance that your FedEx Freight® Priority and FedEx Freight® Economy shipments will arrive on their due date, choose Close of Business Delivery. If you're a standard tariff customer and ship with FedEx Freight Priority service, your shipments are backed by our no-fee money-back guarantee.*

Details About the Service

  • Delivery by 5 p.m. on the standard delivery date.
  • Competitive rates. See the FXF 100 Series Rules Tariff for applicable surcharges.
  • No-fee money-back guarantee for standard rate tariff customers shipping via FedEx Freight Priority.

Choose from two easy options to request Close of Business Delivery for your next shipment:

  • When using a FedEx Freight Bill of Lading, check Close of Business Delivery under Guaranteed Service Options.
  • Clearly write “Close of Business Delivery” in the special instructions of your Bill of Lading and inform your FedEx driver of the earlier-delivery request.

Benefit With Close of Business Delivery

Knowing shipments will arrive by the end of the business day enables you to efficiently plan your workforce and improve inventory management.
