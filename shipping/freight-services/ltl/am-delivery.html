Get delivery by 10:30 a.m.

Early delivery for peace of mind

Ensuring a delivery time of 10:30 a.m. on the scheduled delivery date for your less-than-truckload (LTL) freight shipments can help you plan labor and manufacturing schedules, manage costs and satisfy your customers.1

Details about the service

  • Available with both FedEx Freight® Priority and FedEx Freight® Economy LTL services
  • Supported by a money-back guarantee2
  • See the FXF 100 Series Rules Tariff for applicable surcharges.

Two easy ways to request A.M. delivery

  1. When using a FedEx Freight Bill of Lading, simply check A.M. Delivery under the Guaranteed Service Options.
  2. Clearly write A.M. Delivery in the special instructions section of your Bill of Lading and inform your FedEx driver of the earlier delivery request.

To check your destination ZIP code eligibility, go to fedex.com/amfreight. 


To learn more about FedEx time-definite freight shipping services, contact your account executive or call FedEx Freight Customer Service at 1.866.393.4585.
