FedEx SameDay® Direct
Direct delivery with no additional service-related stops.

Your package will be delivered straight to its destination with no additional service-related stops.

Delivery Time Packages delivered straight to their destination with no additional service-related stops.
Service Days 24 hours a day; 7 days a week; 365 days a year.
Delivery Area Go to fedex.com/samedaycity for available metro markets. Delivery is available between select ZIP codes in each market.
Package Size and Weight Packages up to 150 lbs. each, and up to 65" in length and 120" in length plus girth (length plus width plus height). Total piece count can vary per route.
 Shipment Size and Weight
 Total shipment size is limited to 70 cubic feet, and total shipment weight is limited to 250 lbs. A shipment may consist of up to 25 packages.
Additional Information

You may ship using FedEx SameDay services without an account by calling 1.800.399.5999 and paying via credit card.

To ship online, you must:

1. Have a FedEx account number (open account).

2. Once you have your FedEx account number, go to fedexsameday.com to select "Register for fedexsameday.com" in the new customer center area.

3. Go to fedexsameday.com or call 1.800.399.5999 to request a rate quote or schedule a pickup. Have a government-issued photo ID ready when the courier arrives.
