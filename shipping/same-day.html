Service you can depend on

Need fast shipping options for your time-sensitive packages? Our same-day services provide reliable door-to-door delivery within hours. You can count on:

Delivery by uniformed FedEx couriers in branded vehicles.1

Service backed by the FedEx Money-Back Guarantee.2

Innovative tracking that enables FedEx to see where your delivery vehicle is moment to moment, mile by mile, block by block.1
