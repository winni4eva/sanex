Ship at Office Depot® and OfficeMax®

Ship at Office Depot® and OfficeMax®
Find a Location Find a Location


Now you can conveniently pack and ship FedEx packages at more than 1,500 Office Depot® and OfficeMax® stores nationwide.


Get packed with care.

  • Find a dedicated, self-service packing area with packing and shipping supplies available for purchase in-store.

  • Team members are also ready to help pack your shipment at select locations.


Discover shipping you can count on.

  • Ship with FedEx Express for time-definite delivery with 1-, 2- or 3-day shipping options.

  • Ship with FedEx Ground for fast and reliable day-definite delivery in 1–5 days*.

  • Ship internationally to over 220 countries and territories with FedEx.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I go in-store at Office Depot® and OfficeMax® to pack and ship my FedEx package?

Visit the Copy & Print Center, usually in the front corner of the store.

When are FedEx packing and shipping services available in-store?

Services are offered 7 days a week during normal Office Depot® and Office Max® store hours. Packages shipped or dropped off on days when FedEx Express and FedEx Ground do not pickup will be picked up the next FedEx business day.

Do I need a shipping label before I arrive?

No. You can purchase shipping services inside the store, or create a shipping label before you come into the store.

Are packing and shipping services available at every Office Depot® and OfficeMax® location?

Shipping services are available at all Office Depot® and OfficeMax® locations. Packing services are available at most Office Depot® and OfficeMax® locations.

Do Office Depot® and OfficeMax® locations offer custom packing solutions?

Custom packing solutions are available for certain packages. If an Office Depot® or OfficeMax® store is unable to meet your specific packing needs, please visit one of the 2,000+ FedEx Office locations across the country. To learn more, view the FedEx Service Guide.


*See terms and conditions

*We deliver in 1–5 business days within the 48 states, depending on the distance. Shipments to Alaska, Hawaii and Canada arrive in 3–7 business days. Delivery is day-definite, meaning by the end of the scheduled delivery day.
