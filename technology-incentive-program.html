Keep Up With Technology Innovations

Technology is constantly moving forward, empowering businesses to do the same. But keeping up with the latest innovations isn’t always cost-effective. That’s why FedEx offers direct monetary support to help you get the tech tools you need to grow your business.

With the FedEx Technology Incentive Program, your business can get help paying for integration products that work with your automation systems, as well as cash for printers and servers. The lists of approved vendors gives you the freedom to decide what’s best for your business.

Program Benefits

  • Select from a variety of technology solutions and integration products.
  • Enjoy full purchasing flexibility with cash incentives.
  • Quickly receive incentives with a simple e-sign contract process.
  • Easily prepare labels using FedEx Ship Manager® Server, FedEx Ship Manager® Software or FedEx Web Services.

How It Works

Customer Requirements

  • Must meet eligibility requirements (see your FedEx account executive for details).
  • Must sign a one-, two- or three-year shipping agreement with minimum revenue commitments or participation will be denied.
  • Must agree to make purchase within 10 business days of receipt of funds
  • Customers who terminate will not receive additional incentives for 18 months and may have to repay some or all of their prior incentives.

Get Started

  1. Contact your FedEx account executive to get qualified.
  2. Your account executive will connect you with a program expert who will help you find the best technology recommendation and process the contract.
  3. Review and select a partner from the list of vendors provided.

Warranty

Hardware vendors offer one-, two- or three-year warranties on hardware products and provide technical support, as needed. Additional warranty options are also available for purchase. Please note: FedEx does not provide a warranty or support. Support is provided solely through the hardware vendor.

Choose the Solution That’s Right for You

Sign Up

Speak with your FedEx account executive to see whether you qualify for the FedEx Technology Incentive Program. 

Quickly get your incentives with a simplified e-sign contract process, then choose the products that will help grow your business:

  1. Go to the Integration Vendors and Hardware Vendors tabs on this site for a list of participating vendors.
  2. Click on the vendors’ websites to view product offerings and prices. 
  3. Select a vendor that offers the products you need.
  4. Purchase the product online or call the vendor and identify yourself as a FedEx Technology Incentive Program customer.
  5. Provide the vendor with your FedEx account number and FedEx Technology Incentive Program authorization/contract number.
